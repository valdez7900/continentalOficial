Dry Storage

With over 100,000 square feet of combined warehouse space, Continental Forwarding Services, Grupo Coordinador de Importaciones S.A. de C.V. and Lic. Miguel Angulo have the capacity to handle all of your dry goods storage needs. We operate large facilities in Laredo, Texas, Columbia Nuevo Leon, and Nuevo Laredo Tamaulipas with stack rack capabilities, dock height docks and doors, and 24 hour surveillance systems. While we specialize in cold storage, we are experts in the freight forwarding and storage of dry goods too. Our highly trained staff has been handling the storage, loading, and unloading all types of freight for over 25 years for some of the western hemispheres largest Fortune 500 companies.

Our commitment to customer service, quality, and safety has made Continental Forwarding Services, Grupo Coordinador de Importaciones S.A. de C.V. and Lic. Miguel Angulo the industry leader in the importation and exportation of refrigerated and dry commodities.
